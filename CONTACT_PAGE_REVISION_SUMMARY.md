# Contact Page FAQ Revision Summary

## Overview
The Contact Page's Common Questions section has been comprehensively revised to adopt a more professional, formal tone suitable for enterprise audiences. All questions and answers now reflect polished language, clear communication standards, and refined business-appropriate messaging.

## Changes Made

### 1. Section Header Enhancement
- **Previous:** "Common Questions"
- **New:** "Frequently Asked Questions" with descriptive subtitle
- **Improvement:** More formal terminology with added context line: "Essential information about our services, processes, and engagement models"

### 2. Hero Section Refinement
- **Previous:** "Contact Us" → "Let's Discuss Your Project"
- **New:** "Contact Our Team" → "Let's Explore Your Project Opportunity"
- **Description Enhancement:** Changed from conversational tone to professional, strategic language emphasizing business value and results

### 3. FAQ Questions & Answers Revision

#### Question 1: Project Timeline
- **Previous:** "How long does a typical website project take?"
- **New:** "What is the typical project timeline for website development?"
- **Answer Enhancement:** Added professional terminology like "initiation," "comprehensive timeline projection," "enterprise-level solutions"

#### Question 2: Pricing
- **Previous:** "What are your pricing options?"
- **New:** "How are project fees structured?"
- **Answer Enhancement:** Introduced formal concepts like "pricing model customization," "transparent quotes," "business objectives alignment"

#### Question 3: Hosting & Maintenance
- **Previous:** "Do you provide hosting and maintenance?"
- **New:** "Do you offer hosting infrastructure and ongoing maintenance services?"
- **Answer Enhancement:** More specific terminology: "infrastructure management," "security updates," "scheduled maintenance," "optimal hosting configuration"

#### Question 4: Existing Website Support
- **Previous:** "Can you help with an existing website?"
- **New:** "Do you provide assessment and migration services for existing platforms?"
- **Answer Enhancement:** Changed from casual "take over" to professional "assessment and migration services," "technical audit," "performance gaps," "strategic recommendations"

#### Question 5: Post-Delivery Support
- **Previous:** "What happens after the project is complete?"
- **New:** "What post-delivery support and knowledge transfer are included?"
- **Answer Enhancement:** Emphasized "comprehensive training," "operational control," "flexible ongoing support arrangements," removed casual tone ("You own everything" → "Your organization maintains full ownership")
